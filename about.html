Hi, I’m Maria. I am a Front-End Developer located in Ontario, Canada. I am passionate about 3D animation, motion design and coding. Being a highly energetic person, my interests and hobbies are wide. I love music, playing instruments, singing and performing. Painting and drawing have been part of my life which I have enjoyed greatly. And I also love science, particularly medicine and biology. All these interests, even though wide, have pushed me towards exploring the concepts of animation for medicine and education. I love to create educational material that binds this all together and that will hopefully have a positive impact on the audience.
